About The Federation of Jewish Communities (FJC)
Reviving the past, sustaining the present, and building the future of the Jewish communities in the Former Soviet Union- body and soul.
The FJC is the largest network of social, educational, cultural and religious services supporting Jews and Jewish life across the Former Soviet Union. From large cities to rural villages, we are there, in over 400 communities to fill the needs -physical or spiritual â€“ of Jews in the FSU. We see it as our duty to support the older generation, guide and encourage the current generation, and provide opportunities and education for the next generation. We provide everything necessary to rebuild strengthen and sustain Jewish communal life and culture in the FSU.
