Donate to The Federation of Jewish Communities (FJC)

The FJC is the largest network of social, educational, cultural and religious services supporting Jews and Jewish life across the Former Soviet Union. From large cities to rural villages, we are there, in over 420 communities to fill the needs -physical or spiritual - of Jews in the FSU. We provide everything necessary to rebuild strengthen and sustain Jewish communal life and culture in the FSU.
